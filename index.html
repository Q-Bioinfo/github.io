About Me

I am a scientist and experienced bioinformatician specializing in integrative cancer genomics, multi-omics analysis, and computational biology. I am dedicated to leveraging analytical pipelines for next-generation sequencing and supporting advances in precision oncology.

Research Interests

  • Translational cancer genomics and tumor evolution
  • AI-driven bioinformatics approaches for clinical genomics
  • Liquid biopsy and minimal residual disease detection
  • Single-cell and multi-omics analysis in cancer
  • Extracellular vesicle-mediated signaling in aging and disease
  • Application of next-generation sequencing technologies (WES, WGS, RNA-seq, scRNA-seq) in research and clinical settings
